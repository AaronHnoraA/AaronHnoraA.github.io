About

This site keeps the old personal-homepage sincerity, but the typography, spacing, search, and graph are tuned with current tools rather than nostalgia alone.

This is part homepage, part open notebook. I am a second-year mathematics undergraduate; I write notes here while studying, mostly to force myself to re-derive things until they make sense. If they happen to be useful to anyone else, that is a bonus.

Present focus: quantum computing — quantum states, observables, density operators — together with the algebra and linear algebra it sits on. Theoretical computer science (complexity, algorithms, communication complexity) is the longer horizon; those notes will appear as I write them.
