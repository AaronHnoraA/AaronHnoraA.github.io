About

I am a second-year Mathematics student at UNSW Sydney, with interests in mathematics, quantum computing, and theoretical computer science.

I am a member of the UNSW Science Talented Students Program (TSP), and I currently work with Associate Professor Youming Qiao.

Short Bio

I am currently building a personal archive of mathematical notes, with present emphasis on quantum computing and its algebraic and linear-algebraic foundations.

This site functions both as a study record and as a long-term working notebook for future research in QC and TCS.

Research Areas
